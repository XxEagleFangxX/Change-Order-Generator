FAQ · How-To

01 What's the difference between "COMPLETE" and "ITEMS OPEN" on the PDF?

The PDF header tells the truth about the list: if every item is checked off it reads PUNCH LIST — COMPLETE; if anything's still open it reads ITEMS OPEN, and the sign-off note says the signatures acknowledge the list as walked. Nobody's surprised later.

02 Who signs, and when?

Both of you — contractor and client, right on the screen with a finger. Best time is at the walkthrough: you both saw the same list, you both signed it, and the PDF is the record. You can sign with items still open (the PDF will say so) and generate a fresh COMPLETE copy after close-out.

03 What are the trade tags for?

Tag each item with the trade responsible (paint, electrical, plumbing…) and the PDF groups it all cleanly — so each sub can see their own items at a glance instead of hunting through the whole list.

04 Can I use this on my computer?

Yes — open the same address in your computer's browser. In Chrome or Edge, the install icon at the right end of the address bar puts it on your desktop like a regular app. Data stays per-device; use ⬇ Backup / ⬆ Restore below to move a job between phone and computer.

05 Does it work offline?

Yes. After the first visit it works with zero signal — the walkthrough usually happens in a house with no wifi anyway. Everything runs and saves on your device.

06 Where is my data? What if I switch phones?

Everything lives on this device — no account, no cloud, nothing sent anywhere. Before switching devices, tap ⬇ Backup to save a file, then ⬆ Restore it on the new one.
