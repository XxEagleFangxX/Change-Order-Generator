Privacy Policy

1. Your work stays on your device

Everything you enter into the Change Order Generator app — your business settings, logo, client and job details, line items, signatures, and your full change-order history — is stored only on your own device, in your browser's local storage. It is never transmitted to us or to any server, and there is no account, login, or cloud database. If you clear your browser data or uninstall the app, that information is gone, which is why the app includes a manual Backup/Restore feature you control.

2. Website analytics (cookieless, no personal data)

Our website — the homepage, the Pro page, and the Change Order Generator app at digitalforemansuite.com — uses Vercel Web Analytics to understand basic usage, such as how many people visit or open the app and which pages they view. This analytics is cookieless and does not collect personally identifiable information or track you across other websites. In the app, it counts only that a page was opened (and, where your browser supports it, that the app was added to your home screen) — it never sees anything you type or create. Your business settings, logo, client and job details, line items, signatures, and change-order history always stay on your device, exactly as described in Section 1.

3. Purchases

Pro upgrades are sold and processed by Gumroad, not by us. When you buy Pro, Gumroad handles your payment and email address and issues you a license key. We receive your email and license details only to deliver and support your purchase. We never see or store your full payment-card information. Gumroad's handling of your data is governed by Gumroad's Privacy Policy.

4. Support and bug reports

If you email us, or use the in-app "Report a Bug" button, your message and any details you choose to include are sent to us so we can help you. We use that information only to respond to and improve the product.

5. What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information. We do not build advertising profiles. We do not require an account to use the free app.
